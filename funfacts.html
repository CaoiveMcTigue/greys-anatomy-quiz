Did You Know?

The show's title comes from a real medical textbook.


"Grey's Anatomy" wasn't actually the original title for the show and was changed at least three times.


A real hospital in LA is used for some scenes.


Only three of the main season-one cast members are still on the show.


A background scrub nurse "Bokhee" featured in the show from the beginning is a scrub nurse in real life.


The show consults medical advisors and takes it medical scenes very seriously.


The show uses cow organs for operation scenes.


Some of the stars have also directed episodes.


Sarah Chalke appeared in the season-nine episode "Can't Fight This Feeling" to raise awareness for kawasaki disease, after her son was diagnosed with it and nobody seemed to know what it was.


There have been two spin-off shows from "Grey’s Anatomy". "Private Practise" and "Station 19".


"Grey's Anatomy" scrubs are now used in real hospitals.


The character "Miranda Bailey" is partially inspired by Shonda Rhimes' mom.


Justin Chambers is married with five kids and is nothing like his commitment-phobe character "Alex Karev".


Camilla Luddington, who plays Jo Wilson, is actually British and speaks with a British accent in real life.


Every "Grey's Anatomy" episode title is a song title except for one.


Jesse Williams was a teacher before he played Jackson Avery.


Derek Shepherd was almost played by Rob Lowe. Instead, the beloved McDreamy character was played by Patrick Dempsey.


Pompeo and Dempsey didn't originally know that Derek was married during season-one.


Sarah Drew was really pregnant and gave birth in real life right after filming April's birth scene.


Ellen Pompeo has made millions of dollars for her work on Grey's Anatomy over the years.


Shonda Rhimes has said on more than once occasion that "as long as Pompeo wants to continue to make episodes, the show will go on"
